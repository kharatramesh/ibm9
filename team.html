DOCTORS :
We offer a comprehensive range of fertility treatments and support.
Dr. Shreyas Padgaonkar is the Director of clinic. He completed his
post graduation qualification in Gynaecology from prestigious
G.S. Medical College & K.E.M. Hospital in Mumbai. He completed
fellowship in Reproductive medicine from Reproductive Biology Unit,
The Royal Women’s Hospital, Melbourne, Australia.

He has an exclusive fertility practice since 1993.
He has also helped to establish 16 IVF clinics in India and Abroad.
He has been very active in professional organizations and was the
Secretary General of  ISAR (Indian Society for Assisted Reproduction) in 2008-2010. He has contributed to various books on Infertility and has been a faculty for conferences and workshops.

Dr. Aparna Padgaonkar is Co-founder of the clinic. She completed
her post graduation qualification in Gynaecology and Obstetrics from prestigious G.S. Medical College & K.E.M. Hospital in Mumbai. Both of them lead a team of qualified Doctors, Embryologists and Nurses.

We have a well knit dedicated team of highly qualified professionals
specialized in diverse fields of Gynaecology, Andrology,
Endocrinology, Embryology, Life style modification and
weight management.

We are totally committed to providing the best medical and
scientific services currently available within this field.
We fully appreciate the stresses & anxieties associated with
fertility treatment & offer easy access for couples to information,
understanding and emotional support when most needed.
We encourage both partners to attend the initial consultation
so that both are involved in discussions and decision-making.
